My Favorite Book: Big Magic

Book details

Title
Big Magic
Author
Elizabeth Gilbert
Genre
Non Fiction
Why I love it
This book talks about creative ideas and how it gets passed around from one person to another. If an individual doesn't utilize their idea by taking action in a certain amount of time, it eventually moves on.

My thoughts

Big Magic stood out to me because the author, Elizabeth Gilbert, shares her own experiences with readers about creativity and fleeting ideas of her own..

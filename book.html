My Favorite Book: Big Magic

Book details

Title
Big Magic
Author
Elizabeth Gilbert
Genre
Non Fiction
Why I love it
This book talks about creative ideas and how it gets passed around from one person to another if an individual doesn't utilize the idea by taking action in a certain amount of time.

My thoughts

Big Magic stood out to me because the author, Elizabeth Gilbert, shares her own experiences with readers about creativity and ideas..
